GAME NIGHT EXTRAVAGANZA FT. GLOW AND FOOD BANK

Start March 21, 2014 7:00 pm
End March 21, 2014 11:55 pm
Organizer The Glow Centre for Sexual and Gender Diversity
Venue SLC 2134/2135
Get Directions

Description

Come out and join us for a super fun night of board games! We go beyond monopoly and clue and play some really exciting, head-scratching games. This event is a collaboration between the Glow Centre and the Food Bank to promote a more unified Waterloo community and to also fund-raise non-perishable foods for the Food Bank. This event is open to all students and community members, and is a safe and inclusive space.
