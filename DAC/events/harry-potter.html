Description

Are you a fan Harry Potter movies? Do you enjoy movie marathons? If you said yes to either of these things, join the Waterloo Quidditch Program in watching a Harry Potter Movie Marathon! Admission is a non-perishable food item donation for the Feds Food Bank. Come to watch your favourite Harry Potter movies, or watch them all! Snacks will be on sale.
