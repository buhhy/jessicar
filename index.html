Welcome

Ever since I was little, I've enjoyed all forms of art but never pursued an education in any of them. In fact, I now major in psychology, but work in design.

I am told that I am a very interesting character.
Not in the exciting sense, but rather, in my own mild and boring self.
I don't engage in any unique or extreme sports and hobbies, nor do I spend much time on any one given activity.

Unlike members of my family, I'm a complete introvert.
I don't party, or go out often because socializing is tiring.
However, like my family, I can be very energetic and fun.
My greatest strength and weakness is that I'm too honest, and I often speak my mind.

If you fancy personality types, I've been consistently an INFJ. It fits me to a T.

I'm also a Microsoft fan.
